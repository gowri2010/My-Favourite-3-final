My Hobby Is Playing Music

My hobby is playing Music. Music came into my life when I was 5 years old. My Father bought me a keyboard and that was when Music became my hobby. Music is powerful and calming. It soothens your mind and keeps you at ease. People listen to Music when they are traveling, for fun, and also to go to sleep. Music is like a person you can express your emotions to, because Music is also filled with emotions. Nobody knows how many people listen to Music around the world, but I know for a fact that there are many. Millions of people post videos of songs they sang or made every single day. All of that is Music! One of the best things that have happened in my life is Music. My dream is to become a Musician once I grow up, and create all sorts of pieces of Music.
